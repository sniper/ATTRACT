You are traveling through a far-off galaxy when disaster strikes. Your space ship encounters a critical error, and mistakenly ejects several vital components of the ship. You veer towards an uncharted planet and prepare for a crash landing.

When you awaken, you find yourself atop a series of skyscrapers. Your ship seems salvageable, but it's not going anywhere without those ejected ship parts. Before you begin your search, you grab your Magnet Gun, which allows you to attract to and repel from any magnetic material. This material appears to be common in this abandoned city, and as such this tool will be indispensable in traversing the city. It's time to find those parts and return home...

Inspiration

The gameplay was heavily inspired by the game Portal, as well as the hookshot ability frequently found in the Legend of Zelda games. The environment was inspired by game Mirror's Edge.

User Guide

Menu navigation
  • W / Arrow up = Move selection up
  • S / Arrow down = Move selection down
  • Enter = Select
Gameplay
  • W = Move forward
  • S = Move backward
  • A = Strafe left
  • D = Strafe right
  • Space = Jump
  • Escape = Pause / Unpause
  • Mouse = Aim
  • Left mouse button = Attract to magnet
  • Right mouse button = Repel from magnet
  • Right bracket = Skip level (filthy cheater)
